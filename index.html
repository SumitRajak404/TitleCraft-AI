Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is a YouTube title generator?
A YouTube title generator is an AI-powered tool that creates optimized, high-click-through-rate (CTR) titles for your YouTube videos. It analyzes your topic and generates multiple title options designed to rank in search results and entice viewers to click.
What's the difference between a YouTube title and a thumbnail title?
Your YouTube title is the full SEO-optimized title that appears in search results and below your video (usually 60-70 characters). Your thumbnail title is the short, punchy text overlaid on your thumbnail image — typically 3–7 words designed to grab attention visually at a glance.
Is TitleCraft AI free to use?
Yes! TitleCraft AI is completely free to use. Generate as many YouTube titles and thumbnail text ideas as you need — no sign-up required, no credit card, no limits.
How do I write a good YouTube title for SEO?
A great YouTube SEO title includes your main keyword near the beginning, is between 50–70 characters, creates curiosity or promises clear value, and avoids clickbait without delivery. TitleCraft AI handles all of this automatically based on your topic.
How many title options will I get?
Each generation produces 6 unique title options with different styles — some optimized for SEO ranking, others for emotional hooks, virality, or curiosity. You can regenerate as many times as you like.
